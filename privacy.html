Privacy Policy — Cortes Fire

Last updated: April 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Cortes Fire" application ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when used to publish short-form videos to third-party platforms such as YouTube and TikTok.

1. Information We Collect

The app operates on behalf of a single content creator account (the channel owner) and only accesses data necessary to upload videos and read basic channel metrics. Specifically, the app may access:

  • OAuth access and refresh tokens for the connected TikTok and YouTube accounts
  • Basic public profile information of the connected accounts (username, channel ID)
  • Public performance metrics of videos uploaded by the app (view count, like count, comment count)

2. How We Use Information

Information is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate the channel owner and upload new video content on their behalf
  • Measure the performance of uploaded videos in order to improve future content

The app does not share, sell, or transfer any information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

OAuth tokens and operational data are stored privately on a server controlled by the channel owner. No end-user data is collected because the app is used by a single authenticated operator.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Tokens and logs are retained only as long as necessary to operate the channel. The channel owner may revoke access at any time through the TikTok or YouTube account settings, which immediately invalidates stored tokens.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. It does not knowingly collect information from anyone.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
